Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune 5 on PC with TeknoParrot

January 18, 2020 in Tutorials

After so many years, you can actually play modern arcade titles on PC with TeknoParrot such as Initial D, Blaze Blue, Raiden III and more. In this article, i am going to show how to use TeknoParrot to play one of my favourite arcade titles, Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune 5.

Last updated article: 18th January 2020

First article: 9th March 2019

This post has been updated to the latest version of TeknoParrot and Wangan Terminal.

What is TeknoParrot?

TeknoParrot developed by Teknogods (Well-renowned with their Call Of Duty emulators), allows you to run “selected” modern arcade titles on your own PC. Additionally, it has full support for keyboard and mouse, gamepads, steering wheels and joysticks.

While TeknoParrot is a closed-source project, they also released 2 opensource project called “OpenParrot” and “TeknoParrotUI”, open-source version of TeknoParrot and GUI for both project, respectively.

You can also support Teknogods through Patreon and it allows you to get the latest version of TeknoParrot weeks earlier than public release.

How to use TeknoParrot to play WMMT5?

Requirements:

  • Latest TeknoParrot
  • 7zip or similar
  • Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune 5 Update 5 Eng patched Dump
  • WMMT5 Resolution Patch
  • Gamepad or Steering Wheel (If you use DualShock controllers, you need may need DS4Windows)

Extras:

  • WMMT5 Update 5 Jp Clean Dump
  • Wangan Terminal Project 2.0

To begin with, download all the required files under Requirements“**. You can also choose to download either the English patched dump or the clean japanese dump. However, i would recommend you download the English patched dump. The english translation are based on the international version of WMMT5.

Step 1:

Extract TeknoParrot and your downloaded WMMT5 dump to your preferrable location.

Step 2:

Download WMMT 5 Resolution Patch and extract it into your WMMT5 dump.

Headover to github and download
Extract it to your dump

Step 3:

Run WMMT 5 Resolution Patcher.exe and browse into your WMMT5 dump to select wmn5r.exe. A window will popup which allows you to select your screen resolution (Mine is 2560x1080). Once done, click apply.

Easy peezy!
Select your monitor resolution

Step 4:

Open your TeknoParrot folder and run TeknoParrotUi.exe. TeknoParrot Updater may popup upon launching. Please do download all the updates before proceeding. You may need to restart TeknoParrot after updating.

Step 5:

Once the update is done, a window will inform you that no games are set up. Click Yes, scroll downwards and add WMMT5.

Yes all the way!
Add WMMT5

Step 6:

Next, Click on Game Settings. Once again, under Game Executable, select wmn5r.exe. Additionally, Tick General-XInput and possibly General-WhiteScreenFix too. You can also setup your in-game with General-CustomName. X-Input allows the use of Xbox 360 controllers. WhiteScreenFix fixed the game problem when you tried to Alt+Tab. Finally, click Save Settings.

Step 7:

After that, click on Controller Setup to setup your bindings. Below is my current binding for WMMT 5 with Xbox 360 controller. Don’t forget to click save settings.

Step 8:

Finally, you can start the game!

Extras:

1. How to improve the game graphics?

I have only tried this method on a system with an NVIDIA graphic card.

  1. Right click anywhere in your desktop and select NVIDIA Control Panel

  2. Go to Program Settings, click Add and choose wmn5r.exe

  3. Be sure to choose “High-performance NVIDIA processor” as your preferred graphics processor.

  4. Under Specify the settings for this program, select the following:

Anisotropic filtering : 16x

Antialiasing - FXAA : On

Antialiasing - Mode : Enhance the application setting

Antialiasing - Setting : 32x CSAA

Texture filtering - Quality : High Quality

2. How to use Wangan Terminal Project 2.0?

  1. Extract the contents of Wangan Terminal Project 2.0 to your preferred location.
  2. Launch Wangan Terminal Project 2.0 and wait for the update to complete as the files are huge.
  3. Setup Game Path
  4. Once done, you can do anything you like!

If the Terminal crashes after selecting your WMMT5 directory or on start, delete all the videos inside “Video” folder.

WMMT5 Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

1. Help, my game is running slow despite having a good Nvidia graphic card.

This is possibly due to your system using Integrated graphics by default. To fix this, do the following:

  1. Right click anywhere in your desktop and select NVIDIA Control Panel

  2. Go to Program Settings, click Add and choose wmn5r.exe

  3. Be sure to choose “High-performance NVIDIA processor” as your preferred graphics processor.

2. How do i save my car if there is no Banapassport?

At the end of a race, there will be a selection to either continue the game or not. Selecting “No” will save your car progress.

Cars are saved inside your WMMT5 dump directory at “OpenParrot_Cars”. Do use Wangan Terminal Project 2.0 for dress-up.

3. Why does the story start from the last progress?

Despite using other cars, the game progression will always be the last completed mission. This is due to the Story mode being saved in a single file, located inside WMMT5 dump directory as “openprogress.sav”.

To restart Story mode, delete “openprogress.sav”.

3. Where is Ghost mode?

Currently, there is no Ghost mode. However, progress has been made to enable Ghost mode. For further progress, do check out WMMT5 Online Ghost Project.
